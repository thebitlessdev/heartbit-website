what is heartbit

A physical link between two people, built with electronics and the Internet.

HeartBit always comes in pairs: one device for you and one for someone important. Both connect to the network and are securely linked. Every time one person presses their button, the other heart lights up instantly.

It doesn't replace a message or a call. It's another layer: a simple, silent way to say “I'm here with you” without having to find the right words.

how it works

1 · connection

You connect each HeartBit to your Wi-Fi. They pair through a secure server and are linked together as a pair.

2 · gesture

When you press your button, a signal is sent through the Internet. The other person's HeartBit lights up and beats.

3 · reply

They can reply by pressing their own HeartBit. Then it's your heart that lights up.
